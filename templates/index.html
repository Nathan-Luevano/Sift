About Sift

Sift correlates digital forensic timelines with open-source intelligence data to provide contextual insights for investigations.

  • Parse forensic evidence files (E01, raw images)
  • Collect social media and news data
  • Correlate events temporally and geospatially
  • Visualize relationships and patterns
